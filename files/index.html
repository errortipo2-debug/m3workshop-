Manifesto — 001

Not printed. Not editioned.

Every surface is worked in layers until it settles into something true — then it is never made again. No prints of the originals. No reruns. What leaves the studio does not come back.

Made by hand. In the dark.

M3 Workshop is a solitary practice in abstraction — oxide, ash, oil and acrylic, worked in layers until the surface stops fighting.

Each painting begins from a material, not a plan. Pressure and restraint: how much to build, and exactly when to stop.
